About Medical AI Assistants

Our Mission

Medical AI Assistants is a platform designed to provide AI-powered medical information from multiple specialist perspectives. Our system utilizes advanced language models and a multi-agent approach to analyze medical queries from different medical specialties.

How It Works

When you submit a question, our system performs the following steps:

  1. Emergency Detection: First, we scan for potential medical emergencies and provide appropriate warnings if detected.
  2. Medical Triage: Our AI determines which medical specialties are most relevant to your question.
  3. Multi-Specialist Consultation: We route your question to AI agents specialized in the relevant medical fields.
  4. Consensus Building: The responses from different specialists are combined into a comprehensive answer.
  5. Recommendations: Practical advice and next steps are provided based on the collective expertise.

Technology

Our platform is built using LangGraph, a framework for creating AI agent systems with the following components:

  • Advanced large language models from Groq for generating responses
  • LangGraph for coordinating multiple specialized agents
  • Flask web framework for the user interface
Important Disclaimer

This system is for informational purposes only and is not a replacement for professional medical advice or treatment. The AI assistants provide general information based on their training data, but cannot diagnose conditions or prescribe treatments.

Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for medical concerns.

Development Team
