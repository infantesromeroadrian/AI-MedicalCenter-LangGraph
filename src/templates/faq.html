Frequently Asked Questions

Medical AI Assistants is a platform that provides AI-generated information from multiple medical specialties. It uses advanced AI models and a multi-agent approach with LangGraph to coordinate specialized medical AI agents that work together to answer health-related questions.

No, this system cannot diagnose medical conditions. Medical AI Assistants is designed for informational purposes only and does not provide medical diagnosis, advice, or treatment. It can provide general information about medical topics, but should never be used as a substitute for professional medical care.

Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for any health concerns or medical conditions.

While we strive to provide accurate information, the responses are generated by AI models and may not always be perfectly accurate or complete. The system:

  • Uses advanced language models trained on medical information
  • Employs multiple specialized agents to provide different perspectives
  • Applies confidence scoring to identify when a response might be uncertain

However, AI models can make mistakes or provide incomplete information. Always verify important medical information with healthcare professionals.

  • Ask clear, specific questions about medical topics
  • Provide relevant context about your question when needed
  • Use the system to gather information, not for diagnosis
